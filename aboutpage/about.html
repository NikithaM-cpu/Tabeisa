Tabeisa StartUp Cafe

“Tabeisa (Technical and Business Education Initiative in South Africa) has launched a Start Up Café at the Cape Peninsula University of Technology to assist aspiring entrepreneurs to start and manage their businesses” – Cape Peninsula University of Technology, 2007. Tabeisa Start UP Café is a platform where the CPUT community can learn entrepreneurial skills and start their entrepreneurial journey by opening their own businesses or projects and be able to maintain them through the different project life cycle.
